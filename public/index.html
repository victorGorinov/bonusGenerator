License Rules

Every Bonus Configured for Your Jurisdiction

Bonus caps, wager limits, mandatory regulatory strings, and self-exclusion checks — each license has its own rules. Retomat knows them and applies them automatically.

MGA
Malta / EU
Standard
  • Wager 20–40× typical range
  • Responsible gambling tools required
  • T&Cs must be clearly accessible
  • No misleading winning probability claims
UKGC
United Kingdom
Strict
  • Max £10 per spin / bet while wagering
  • Gamstop self-exclusion check required
  • BeGambleAware.org reference mandatory
  • No countdown timers on active bonuses
DGA
Denmark
Strict
  • Hard cap: 1,000 DKK per bonus offer
  • Minimum validity: 60 days
  • ROFUS self-exclusion check required
  • T&Cs same font size as promotional headline
Curaçao
Global
Permissive
  • No statutory bonus or wager cap
  • Basic KYC & AML compliance required
  • T&Cs must be published on site
  • No mandatory self-exclusion registry
Anjouan
Global
Permissive
  • No statutory bonus or wager cap
  • Basic KYC required, no wagering mandate
  • Responsible gambling disclaimer recommended
  • Fewer enforcement precedents than Curaçao
Kahnawake
Canada / Global
Standard
  • No statutory bonus or wager cap
  • Player dispute resolution mechanism required
  • KYC & AML compliance required
  • No mandatory self-exclusion registry
Gibraltar

Standard
  • Wager requirements must be fair & clear
  • Responsible gambling tools required
  • No statutory bonus cap
  • GRA monitors for unfair bonus terms
