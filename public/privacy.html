Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 May 2025  ·  Effective date: 20 May 2025

🔒 Short version: We collect your name, work email, and role when you sign up for early access. We do not sell your data, use advertising trackers, or set third-party cookies. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

1. Who We Are

Retomat ("we", "us", "our") is a B2B software platform providing AI-powered bonus configuration and CRM campaign generation tools for iGaming operators. Our services are directed exclusively at iGaming industry professionals.

Contact: hello@retomat.io

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data when you submit the early access form on our website:

  • Name — to personalise communications
  • Work email address — to send access confirmation and product updates
  • Role / job title — to understand our user base and tailor onboarding

We do not collect payment data, passport information, IP addresses, or any sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Send you early access confirmation and onboarding information
  • Notify you of product updates, new features, and beta announcements
  • Understand aggregate demand by industry role

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process your data on the basis of legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) — specifically, providing the B2B service you signed up for and communicating product-related information relevant to your professional role. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Storage and Processors

Your signup data is processed by Resend (resend.com), our transactional email provider. Resend is GDPR-compliant and processes data within the EU/EEA. We do not store personal data in any other third-party database.

No other third-party data processors receive your personal information.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

Our website uses browser localStorage only — not cookies — to remember your language preference (EN/RU). This is strictly functional and does not track you across sites or sessions beyond language preference.

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or any analytics trackers
  • Facebook Pixel or advertising pixels
  • Session tracking or fingerprinting
  • Any third-party cookies

7. Data Retention

We retain your contact details for as long as Retomat operates as a product or until you request deletion, whichever comes first. You can request immediate deletion at any time.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — ask us to limit processing of your data
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@retomat.io. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Security

All data in transit is encrypted via TLS. Access to our systems is restricted to authorised personnel. We implement security headers (CSP, HSTS, X-Frame-Options) on all web endpoints.

10. Children

Retomat is a professional B2B tool intended solely for adults working in the iGaming industry. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our product evolves. Material changes will be communicated by email to registered users. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

12. Contact

For any privacy-related queries or data requests: hello@retomat.io
